About MagicMemo

MagicMemo is an innovative app that uses advanced AI technology to assist in memorizing English texts. With various features powered by artificial intelligence, MagicMemo makes learning more effective and fun!

Key Features

OCR Scanning & Sync Across Devices

MagicMemo uses Optical Character Recognition (OCR) to scan text, and all your items will sync seamlessly across all your devices. This ensures you can access your memorization data wherever you are.

Random Blank Features

Fill in the blanks by spelling the correct word. MagicMemo also supports Fill-in-the-Blanks with word/form choice options, helping you memorize quickly and efficiently.

AI Recitation Engine

MagicMemo incorporates the latest Transformer speech neural network for recitation, providing a powerful tool for memorization and practice.

Offline Functionality

MagicMemo relies on Apple's powerful processor, allowing the app to work completely offline. No internet connection is required to enjoy all features of the app.
