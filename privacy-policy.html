Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to MagicMemo. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data, including the use of CloudKit and Microsoft TTS services, and assures you that we take every measure to safeguard your privacy.

Data Collection

MagicMemo does not collect, store, or share any personal information unless explicitly required for specific features of the app.

However, in order to sync your data across devices and offer certain features, such as text-to-speech functionality, we may upload certain data to Apple's **CloudKit** or send user input to **Microsoft's TTS (Text-to-Speech) service**.

Third-Party Services

MagicMemo uses third-party services to enhance your experience:

  • CloudKit: MagicMemo uses Apple's **CloudKit** service to sync your data across devices. This ensures that all your memorization data, including OCR results and learning progress, are seamlessly accessible across your devices. The data stored in CloudKit is encrypted and protected by Apple’s privacy policies.
  • Microsoft TTS Service: When you use the text-to-speech functionality, user-entered text may be transmitted to Microsoft's TTS service for processing. This service uses advanced AI technology to generate speech from text. The data may be stored temporarily by Microsoft in order to process the request and generate audio. Please note that Microsoft’s privacy policy applies to this service.

We do not collect or store any personal information without your explicit consent, and we strive to keep all data secure and private.

Data Protection and Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data. However, please note that no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your data while using third-party services like CloudKit and Microsoft TTS.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information. If you wish to exercise these rights, or if you have concerns about data stored in **CloudKit** or processed by **Microsoft TTS**, please contact us at the email provided below.
